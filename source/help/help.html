Getting Started

How to access PC4U and set up your account.

1

Visit the Site

Open your browser and navigate to the PC4U website. You will land on the home page, where you can explore the site as a visitor or log in to access full features.

2

Create an Account or Log In

Click LOG IN or REGISTER in the top-right corner of the navigation bar. Authentication is handled securely through Auth0.

  • Click REGISTER to create a new account with your email.
  • Click LOG IN if you already have an account.
  • Once logged in, your name will appear in the top-right corner of the navbar.
  • Click LOGOUT to sign out at any time.
3

Navigate the Site

Use the navigation bar at the top to move between sections of the site:

🏠 Home — Budget quiz & build finder
🖥️ Builds — Sample PC builds
🛒 Shop — Parts store
💬 Discussion — Community board
👥 About Us — Team info
🆘 Support — This page

Budget Quiz

Tell us your budget and we'll instantly recommend the right PC build for you.

1

Start the Quiz

On the home page, click the START button to begin. The intro text will animate away and the budget quiz will appear.

2

Set Your Budget

Drag the slider to set your budget between $300 and $2000+. Your current budget is shown above the slider in real time.

3

Get Your Recommendation

As you move the slider, PC4U instantly recommends a build based on your budget:

💻 $300–$699 — Beginner Build
🖥️ $700–$1199 — Advance Build
🚀 $1200+ — Professional Build
Example

Set your budget to $900 → PC4U recommends the Advance Build — more performance for higher FPS gaming and moderate work.

4

View the Build or Build Your Own

Once you have a recommendation, you have two options:

  • Click VIEW THIS BUILD to go directly to the full specs and parts list for your recommended build.
  • Click BUILD YOUR OWN to configure a custom build from scratch. (Coming soon — not yet functional.)

Builds

Browse sample PC builds curated by the PC4U team.

1

Browsing Builds

The Builds page displays a collection of pre-configured PC builds at various price points and performance levels. Each card shows a snapshot of the build including its name, category, and key specs.

2

View Details Work in Progress

Each build card has a View Details button that opens a detailed view of the build. Component information is still being added and may be incomplete.

Discussion Board

A community space to ask questions, share tips, and talk PC.

1

Browsing Posts (Visitors & Logged-In Users)

Anyone can browse the discussion board without logging in. You can search for posts by keyword and sort results by:

🕐 Latest
❤️ Most Likes
💬 Most Comments
Example

Search for "GPU upgrade" and sort by Most Liked to find the most popular community advice on GPU upgrades.

2

Creating a Post 🔒 LOGIN REQUIRED

Logged-in users can create new posts to ask questions or share information with the community.

  • Log in using the LOG IN button in the navbar.
  • Navigate to the Discussion page and click Create Post.
  • Enter a title and write your message in the body field.
  • Submit the post — it will appear on the board immediately.
  • You can delete your own posts at any time.
3

Replying to Posts 🔒 LOGIN REQUIRED

Logged-in users can reply to any post. Replies support nested threading, so you can reply directly to another reply.

  • Click on a post to open it.
  • Scroll to the reply section and type your response.
  • To reply to a specific comment, click the Reply button on that comment.
  • You can delete your own replies at any time.
4

Reacting to Posts 🔒 LOGIN REQUIRED

Logged-in users can like or dislike any post to show appreciation or disagreement.

❤️ Like a post
👎 Dislike a post
5

What Visitors Can & Cannot Do

✅ View all posts
✅ Search posts
✅ Sort posts
🔒 Create posts
🔒 Reply to posts
🔒 Like / dislike
